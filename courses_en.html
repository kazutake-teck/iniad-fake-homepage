Four courses of INIAD

During the first year, all students immerse themselves in intensive learning of programming, as well as Japanese and English communication, establishing the foundation for information integration.
From the second year onward, students diverge into four distinct courses, each dedicated to specialized lectures and exercises tailored to the respective field of study.

  • INIAD Engineering Course
  • INIAD Design Course
  • INIAD Business Course
  • INIAD Civil System Course
